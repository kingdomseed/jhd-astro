Released March 24, 2025.

v1.4.6 focused on navigation polish, localization fixes, launcher integration,
and general stability work across the app.

## Highlights

- App drawer navigation is more consistent across supported languages.
- The app now supports a URL scheme for Essential Launcher and other third-party
  launcher integrations.
- Localization fixes addressed display issues in translated views.
- Error reporting and general performance were improved.

## Changed

- Journal validation was strengthened for better stability.
- Fate Chart display is more reliable with different language configurations.
- Android builds gained support for faster updates through Shorebird.

## Recent v1.4.5 Additions

- Complete Portuguese translation with tables and interface text.
- Spanish, French, and Italian UI-only language support.
- Evil Deed table and Mythic Magazine 49 content.
- Better accessibility support for screen readers.
