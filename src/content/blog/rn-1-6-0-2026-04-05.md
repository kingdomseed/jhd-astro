This is the biggest feature release since the app launched. Three systems from the Mythic GME 2e rules that players have been requesting — Progress Tracks, Discovery Checks, and Keyed Scenes — are all landing together. The Game Panel is getting a full redesign to hold them: a tabbed layout with Meaning Tables, Event Focus, Tracks, and Discovery each in their own tab.

Each one went through a few design iterations before I was happy with how they felt in play. Here's what's new.

## Highlights

- **Progress Tracks**: Visual progress bars for tracking thread advancement. Set custom point ranges, mark Flashpoints in the Adventure Log, and watch the bar fill as the story moves forward. Controls use a two-tap "armed action" pattern — tap once to arm, tap again to confirm — so you don't accidentally bump your progress.
- **Discovery Checks**: A dedicated panel for the oracle-style Discovery Check mechanic. Roll, get an automated result summary, and (if you have Progress Tracks active) the result auto-applies to the right track. Localized across all 8 languages.
- **Keyed Scenes**: Set up scene triggers that fire when conditions are met. Choose count-based targets with a visual stepper, pick one-shot (deactivates after trigger) or recurring (resets and stays active), and let the app auto-trigger when the count reaches the target. Triggered scenes get a badge and a dedicated log entry.
- **Hybrid Play**: Real-dice prompts are now available across Fate rolls, Scene checks, Event Focus, Meaning Tables, Adventure Lists, and the Dice Roller. Physical dice can stay part of play without losing app interpretation or logging.
- **Tabbed Game Panel**: The old single-panel view is gone. Meaning Tables, Event Focus, Tracks, and Discovery now live in their own tabs — less scrolling, faster switching.

## Added

- **Progress Tracks**: Thread progress tracking with visual progress bars, custom point ranges, Flashpoint log entry support, and correction controls so long-running storylines are easier to guide toward a conclusion.
- **Discovery Checks**: Dedicated Discovery Check panel with oracle-style outcomes, automated result summaries, automatic track updates when appropriate, and guidance when checks are blocked or reset. Localized across all 8 languages.
- **Keyed Scenes**: Triggered scene badges, inline auto-increment toggles, count-based targets (one-shot and recurring), auto-trigger support, and dedicated game log builders for recurring threats and climactic moments.
- **Hybrid Play**: Opt-in real-dice prompts across Fate rolls, Scene checks, Event Focus, Meaning Tables, Adventure Lists, and the Dice Roller — physical dice can stay part of play without losing app interpretation or logging.
- **Game Panel**: Refactored into a tabbed layout housing Meaning Tables, Event Focus, Progress Tracks, Discovery Checks, and Keyed Scenes with richer help sheets and more polished controls across layouts.

## Changed

- **Game Panel**: Meaning Tables, Event Focus, Progress Tracks, Discovery Checks, and Keyed Scenes now live in a cleaner tabbed panel with richer help sheets and more polished controls across layouts.
- **Premium**: Mobile subscription and renewal flows now stay inside the app, with clearer upgrade messaging and a more accurate localized roadmap.
- **Feature Polish**: Track controls, Discovery Check results, and Keyed Scene actions were simplified so the new 1.6 tools are easier to read and use at a glance.

## Fixed

- **First Launch**: Fresh installs now show privacy consent and current release notes in the right order, while avoiding redundant setup work before the app becomes interactive.
- **iCloud Sync**: First-time cloud enable, offline handling, reachability checks, and ongoing status updates are more reliable and less prone to hanging.
- **Stability**: Fixed a class of stale-dismiss and keyboard-transition issues across dialogs, sheets, and shared flows to reduce accidental double actions and crashes.
